About Us

The story behind Hot Shot Bakery

Our Story

Hot Shot Bakery was founded in 2010 by Sarah Omar, a passionate baker who started selling bread from her kitchen in Nairobi. What began as a small home operation quickly grew into one of the most loved bakeries in the city.

Today, we operate a full bakery with a team of 12 dedicated bakers, serving hundreds of customers every day. Our mission is simple — bring joy through food.

Our Values

Quality

Every item is made with the best available produce.

Community

We source locally and give back to the Nairobi community that has supported us.

Happiness

We believe good food brings people together. That's why we bake with joy every day.
